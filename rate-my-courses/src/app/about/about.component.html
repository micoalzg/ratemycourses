 Instructions

1. Register and login to an account.
2. View reviews for a course by typing it into the searchbar at the top of any page.
3. Add reviews by clicking the "+" icon on the right of the navbar.
4. Add courses that don't exist in the database by searching for it, or by pressing the pencil icon in the courses box in your dashboard.
5. Leave feedback for reviews (like/dislike, report) by clicking the three dots on the top right corner of a review.
6. Leave suggestions for the app by pressing the user icon on the right of the navbar.
